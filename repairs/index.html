Combination Meter (Instrument Cluster)

Speedometer drops to 0 while driving. Fuel gauge stuck. Odometer frozen. A capacitor repair can save you $400+ over dealer replacement.

Medium

Hybrid System

🔋

Hybrid Battery Pack

Red triangle, HV battery warning, power loss. The biggest repair most Gen 2 owners will face — but it's not always as expensive as you think.

Hard
💧

Inverter Coolant Pump

One of the best preventive repairs you can do. This $80 pump keeps your inverter alive. Replacing it proactively costs less than one hour of tow fees.

Easy

Lighting

💡

Headlight Bulb Replacement

No tools needed. The push-down-then-forward trick everyone misses the first time. Bulb recommendations and YouTube guides.

Easy

Brakes

🛑

ABS Actuator

ABS, VSC, and brake lights all on at once — the "3 amigos." The actuator pump runs constantly and drains the battery. Dealer wants $1,500. There are better options.

Hard
